Fail Case Manual

Several possible functions that represent the cross-sections or equivalently the Prandtl stress functions were tested and their results were designated as a "PASS" or a "FAIL" depending on the App's response to the input functions. The working of the algebra is explicitly shown and the conclusions and observations have been pointed out. Assumptions, limitations of the app, some notable points and a the listing of the cases are also available for the user's quick access to the final results.
